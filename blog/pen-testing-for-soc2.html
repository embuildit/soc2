Penetration Testing and SOC 2

A Deep Dive into Strengthening Your Security Posture

Is Penetration Testing Required for SOC 2?

While not explicitly mandatory for all SOC 2 audits, penetration testing is strongly recommended and often expected by auditors and clients, especially for the 'Security' TSC. It serves as a powerful validation of your vulnerability management program (CC4.1).

What Does a SOC 2 Pen Test Involve?

A penetration test for SOC 2 is a simulated cyber attack against your systems to identify and exploit vulnerabilities. The scope should align with your SOC 2 system description and typically includes:

  • Network and application vulnerability scanning
  • Manual testing to exploit identified vulnerabilities
  • A detailed report with findings, risk levels, and remediation recommendations
