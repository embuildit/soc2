SOC 2 costs vary dramatically based on your approach. The ranges below reflect real-world data from 200+ companies who completed SOC 2 audits in 2024.

Detailed Cost Components

1. Audit Fees (40-60% of total cost)

Professional fees charged by your CPA firm:

  • Big Four (Deloitte, PwC, KPMG, EY): $40,000 - $100,000+
  • Top Tier (A-LIGN, Schellman): $15,000 - $60,000
  • Regional Firms: $8,000 - $30,000
  • Boutique Specialists: $5,000 - $25,000
Hidden Audit Costs: Many firms charge extra for scope changes, additional testing, or remediation support. Always ask for a fixed-fee proposal.

2. Automation Platform (20-40% of total cost)

Annual subscription for compliance automation:

  • Vanta: $9,500 - $25,000+/year
  • Drata: $7,000 - $20,000+/year
  • Secureframe: $5,000 - $15,000+/year
  • Strike Graph: $6,000 - $18,000+/year
  • Thoropass: $5,000 - $12,000+/year

3. Internal Resources (10-30% of total cost)

Often overlooked but significant:

  • Project Manager: 20-40% time for 6-12 months
  • IT/DevOps: 10-20% time for implementation
  • Security Team: 15-30% time for controls
  • Legal/Compliance: 5-15% time for policies

Cost range: $20,000 - $80,000 in internal time

4. Additional Tools & Infrastructure (5-15% of total cost)

  • Security tools: $2,000 - $10,000/year
  • Monitoring solutions: $1,000 - $5,000/year
  • Access management: $2,000 - $8,000/year
  • Backup solutions: $500 - $3,000/year

Cost by Approach

DIY Approach

Total Cost: $15,000 - $40,000

  • ✅ Lowest upfront cost
  • ❌ Highest time investment (6-18 months)
  • ❌ Higher risk of audit failures
  • ❌ Significant internal expertise required

Automation Platform + Budget Auditor

Total Cost: $25,000 - $80,000

  • ✅ Balanced cost and efficiency
  • ✅ Faster implementation (3-6 months)
  • ✅ Reduced internal workload
  • ❌ Platform learning curve

Premium Platform + Top Tier Auditor

Total Cost: $50,000 - $150,000

  • ✅ Highest success rate
  • ✅ Fastest time to compliance
  • ✅ Best customer perception
  • ❌ Highest cost

Consulting-Heavy Approach

Total Cost: $75,000 - $200,000+

  • ✅ Minimal internal effort
  • ✅ Expert guidance throughout
  • ❌ Highest total cost
  • ❌ Less internal knowledge transfer

Hidden Costs to Watch For

Scope Creep

  • Additional Trust Service Criteria: +$5,000 - $15,000 each
  • Multiple locations: +$3,000 - $10,000 per location
  • Complex integrations: +$2,000 - $8,000

Remediation Costs

  • Failed controls requiring fixes: $5,000 - $25,000
  • Additional audit time: $150 - $400/hour
  • Emergency consulting: $200 - $500/hour

Ongoing Compliance (Annual)

  • Platform renewals: $5,000 - $25,000
  • Annual re-audits: 50-75% of initial audit cost
  • Continuous monitoring: $2,000 - $10,000
  • Internal maintenance: $10,000 - $30,000

Cost Optimization Strategies

1. Start Early

Beginning compliance efforts 6-12 months before your target date reduces rush fees and emergency consulting costs.

2. Choose the Right Auditor

Big Four names impress customers but cost 2-3x more. Boutique specialists often provide better value and attention.

3. Leverage Automation

Platform costs seem high upfront but reduce total implementation time and ongoing maintenance significantly.

4. Bundle Services

Many platforms offer auditor partnerships with discounted combined pricing.

5. Negotiate Fixed Fees

Avoid hourly billing for audit work. Get fixed-fee proposals to control costs.
