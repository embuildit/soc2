In-depth analysis of OneTrust GRC pricing and cost-effective alternatives

July 31, 2025 8 min read

Platform Status Update

Tugboat Logic was acquired by OneTrust in 2021. The former Tugboat Logic platform is now integrated into OneTrust's comprehensive GRC suite. This analysis covers 2025 pricing for OneTrust GRC, which includes the compliance automation capabilities previously offered by Tugboat Logic.

OneTrust GRC Pricing Overview

OneTrust has transitioned from Tugboat Logic's simpler pricing model to a more complex enterprise-focused structure. Understanding the 2025 pricing requires examining multiple components and factors that influence the total cost.

Hidden Costs and Additional Fees

Often Overlooked Expenses

  • Data storage: $500 - $2,000/month for large evidence volumes

Annual Increases

OneTrust typically implements annual price increases of 3-8%, with larger increases during contract renewals based on:

  • Feature enhancements and new capabilities
  • Market positioning adjustments
  • Increased usage and data volume
  • Enterprise feature adoption
