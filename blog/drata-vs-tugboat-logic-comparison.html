Drata vs Tugboat Logic: Platform Battle

Comprehensive comparison of two powerful SOC 2 compliance automation solutions

July 31, 2025 11 min read

Platform Context

Tugboat Logic was acquired by OneTrust in 2021 and integrated into the OneTrust GRC platform. This comparison evaluates Drata against the current OneTrust offering that incorporates former Tugboat Logic capabilities.

Platform Overview

Drata

The Multi-Framework Automation Expert

  • Founded: 2020
  • Focus: Multi-framework compliance automation
  • Market: Mid-market to Enterprise
  • Approach: Evidence-first automation
  • Headquarters: San Diego, CA
  • Customers: 4,000+ companies

OneTrust (Tugboat Logic)

The Comprehensive GRC Platform

  • Founded: 2017 (acquired 2021)
  • Focus: Integrated GRC ecosystem
  • Market: Enterprise-focused
  • Approach: Comprehensive governance suite
  • Headquarters: Atlanta, GA
  • Customers: 14,000+ organizations

Drata's Key Advantages

Multi-Framework Excellence

Built from the ground up for multiple compliance frameworks. Single platform for SOC 2, ISO 27001, PCI DSS, HIPAA, and more with unified controls.

Superior Evidence Automation

Industry-leading evidence collection with 120+ integrations. Automated evidence mapping and continuous monitoring reduce manual work by 80%.

Advanced Risk Management

Sophisticated risk assessment and scoring. Real-time risk monitoring with predictive analytics for proactive compliance management.

Customer Success Focus

Dedicated customer success managers and compliance experts. High customer satisfaction scores and responsive support team.

OneTrust's Key Advantages

Enterprise Integration

Seamless integration with existing OneTrust privacy and GRC modules. Single vendor for comprehensive governance, risk, and compliance needs.

Deep Customization

Extensive customization capabilities for enterprise requirements. Custom workflows, controls, and reporting to match organizational needs.

Global Scale

Proven at enterprise scale with global deployment capabilities. Multi-tenant architecture supports complex organizational structures.

Market Leadership

OneTrust's market-leading position in privacy and GRC provides credibility and stability for long-term compliance programs.

Use Case Scenarios

When Drata is the Better Choice

Multi-Framework Requirements

Scenario: 400-person SaaS company needs SOC 2, ISO 27001, and PCI DSS

  • Multiple compliance requirements from different customers
  • Want unified platform for all frameworks
  • Need efficient evidence collection across standards
  • Budget $30,000-60,000 annually

Why Drata wins: Purpose-built for multi-framework compliance with superior evidence automation and clear pricing.

Fast-Growing Mid-Market

Scenario: 200-person company scaling rapidly with increasing compliance demands

  • Growing from first SOC 2 to multiple frameworks
  • Need platform that scales with growth
  • Want compliance-focused solution
  • Value excellent customer support

Why Drata wins: Scalable multi-framework approach, excellent customer success, and compliance specialization.

When OneTrust is the Better Choice

Large Enterprise Organizations

Scenario: 3,000+ employee company with comprehensive GRC needs

  • Need privacy management alongside compliance
  • Complex organizational structure
  • Existing OneTrust privacy deployments
  • Budget $100,000+ for comprehensive GRC

Why OneTrust wins: Comprehensive GRC platform, enterprise scale, existing privacy integrations, and deep customization.

Complex Global Operations

Scenario: Multinational corporation with diverse compliance requirements

  • Operations across multiple jurisdictions
  • Need vendor risk management integration
  • Require extensive customization
  • Have dedicated GRC teams

Why OneTrust wins: Global scale, comprehensive feature set, vendor risk integration, and enterprise customization capabilities.

Final Recommendation

Decision Matrix

Choose Drata if you:

  • Need multiple compliance frameworks (SOC 2, ISO 27001, PCI)
  • Are a mid-market company (100-1,000 employees)
  • Want specialized compliance automation
  • Value transparent pricing and fast implementation
  • Prioritize evidence automation and continuous monitoring

Choose OneTrust if you:

  • Are a large enterprise (1,000+ employees)
  • Need comprehensive GRC beyond just compliance
  • Already use OneTrust for privacy management
  • Require extensive customization and integration
  • Have dedicated GRC teams and resources

Bottom line: Drata excels at multi-framework compliance automation for growing companies, while OneTrust provides comprehensive enterprise GRC capabilities for large organizations with complex governance needs.
