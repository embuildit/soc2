SOC 2 and GDPR

A Guide to Integrated Data Protection Compliance

The Overlap Between SOC 2 and GDPR

While SOC 2 is a framework for auditing controls and GDPR is a data privacy regulation, there is significant overlap, particularly with the 'Privacy' TSC. Many of the controls you implement for SOC 2 can help you meet GDPR requirements.

Leveraging SOC 2 for GDPR

  • Data Governance: SOC 2's emphasis on data classification and handling aligns with GDPR's principles of data minimization and purpose limitation.
  • Access Controls: Strong access controls, a cornerstone of SOC 2, are essential for protecting personal data as required by GDPR.
  • Incident Response: A robust incident response plan, required for SOC 2, is also a key requirement of GDPR's data breach notification rules.
