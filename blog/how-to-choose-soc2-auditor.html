How to Choose the Right SOC 2 Auditor

A 10-Point Checklist for a Successful Partnership

The Checklist

  1. Experience with your industry and technology stack.
  2. Reputation and accreditation (AICPA member).
  3. Clear communication and a defined process.
  4. A collaborative approach, not just an audit.
  5. Transparent pricing and a detailed scope of work.
  6. Experience with companies of your size and stage.
  7. Positive client references and testimonials.
  8. A modern, technology-driven audit process.
  9. The ability to grow with you and offer other services.
  10. A good cultural fit with your team.
